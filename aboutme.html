Let me tell you a story about CaffeiNing.

It all started a decade ago in Shanghai with a simple cup of pour-over coffee made from Ethiopia Yega SOE, washed processed. From that moment on, my fascination with coffee blossomed into a full-blown passion. I have since embarked on a journey to explore the rich tapestry of flavors and aromas that coffee has to offer.

Venturing into various cafes has become a beloved pastime, where I immerse myself in their signature brews and engagingin enlightening conversations with talented baristas. Through these activities, I have leaned invaluable insights and knowledge about the artistry behind each cup.

Driven by a thirst for understanding, I have delved deep into the intricacies of coffee. From learning about its diverse origins and varieties to mastering sensory evaluation and cupping techniques, I have eagerly absorbed every aspect of this beloved beverage. My curiosity also led me to explore different brewing methods and the regular coffee drinks.

At first, I shied away from documenting my coffee adventures, believing that true appreciation should be experienced with all the senses. However, as my journey progressed, I came to realize the importance of preserving these moments and insights. Thus, coffeining was born - a space to not only record down my experiences but also to share my passion and ideas with other coffee enthusiasts.
